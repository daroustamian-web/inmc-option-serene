/ 03 Dr. Amaliya Santiago, ND [ 03 ]
INMC Medical Director

Dr. Amaliya Santiago, ND

Dr. Amaliya Santiago's choice of a future profession as a natural medicine doctor was molded early, having been raised in a family of doctors and other medical professionals. However, her passion for combining the traditional methodologies of western medicine with holistic, naturopathic protocols was due to her own challenges of helping her own children overcome issues with autoimmune diseases.

Driven to find answers for facilitating long-term benefits for both her children and her patients, Dr. Santiago began a journey that would lead her to establish Los Angeles' Integrative Natural Medical Clinic.

After receiving dual degrees in Biology and Political Science from California State University, Los Angeles, Dr. Santiago became a certified diagnostic ultrasound technician and went on to bolster her medical education by studying Medical Hydrotherapy in Germany. She earned her medical degree in 2015 from Washington's prestigious Bastyr University.

As founder of INMC, Dr. Amaliya Santiago has worked to create a comprehensive wellness program that combines science-backed, proven methodologies that holistically treat patients of all ages. Designing customized treatment plans that target each patient's specific healthcare needs, Dr. Santiago and the rest of the INMC staff strive to restore the body's natural responses and functions.
