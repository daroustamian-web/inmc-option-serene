/ 05 FAQ

Before Your First Visit

Schedule a 15-Min Discovery Call

Do you accept insurance?

We are an out-of-network provider for most insurance plans. We provide superbills that you can submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement. Many of our patients receive partial reimbursement through their PPO plans. We accept all major credit cards and offer payment plans for treatment protocols.

What does the initial visit involve?

Your first visit includes a thorough review of your health history, current symptoms, and wellness goals. Dr. Santiago takes time to listen and understand the full picture before recommending a personalized treatment protocol. Depending on your needs, she may order lab work to identify root causes before beginning any treatment.

How long does an EBOO session take?

A typical EBOO (Extracorporeal Blood Oxygenation and Ozonation) session takes approximately 45 minutes to one hour. During the treatment, blood is drawn, filtered, oxygenated, ozonated, and returned to the body. Most patients relax comfortably during the session and can resume normal activities afterward.

What conditions do you treat?

Dr. Santiago treats a wide range of conditions including chronic fatigue, autoimmune disorders, Lyme disease, cancer support (complementary to conventional treatment), hormonal imbalances, chronic pain, digestive issues, inflammatory conditions, mold exposure, heavy metal toxicity, and overall wellness optimization. Her approach focuses on identifying and addressing root causes rather than managing symptoms.

What is the 15-minute discovery call?

It's a brief phone conversation with Dr. Santiago to discuss your health concerns and goals. This helps both you and the doctor determine if her integrative approach is the right fit for your situation. There's no pressure and no commitment — just an honest conversation about whether we can help.
